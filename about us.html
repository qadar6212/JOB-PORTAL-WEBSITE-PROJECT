Our 30-Year Journey

From a local newspaper listing to a global digital platform.

1994

The Beginning

JobNest was founded as a small recruitment agency in New York, focused on connecting local businesses with finance professionals.

2004

Going Digital

We launched JobNest.com, moving from paper-based recruiting to a fully online job board, expanding our reach across the USA.

2014

Global Expansion

Opened offices in London, Singapore, and Berlin. We introduced mobile applications to make applying on-the-go easier than ever.

2020

AI Integration

Launched our "Smart Match" algorithm, using Artificial Intelligence to perfectly pair candidates' skills with employer needs.

2024

Industry Leader

Celebrating 30 years of excellence. Today, JobNest is the #1 trusted source for jobs in Tech, Finance, and Healthcare worldwide.
